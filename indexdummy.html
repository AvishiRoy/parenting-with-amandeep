About Amandeep

Meet Amandeep Sethi

Amandeep Sethi is a parenting coach, counsellor and workshop facilitator dedicated to helping families build secure, emotionally healthy relationships. Her work centres on conscious parenting — an approach that focuses on understanding a child's emotional needs rather than simply managing their behaviour.

Over the past 18 years, she has worked closely with parents, schools and communities across India, conducting 1200+ workshops and guiding over 5000 parents through the everyday challenges of raising children. Her sessions blend practical, research-informed strategies with genuine empathy, giving parents tools they can use the same day, not just theory to think about later.

Whether she's working with a single family or a room full of educators, Amandeep's goal stays the same: to help adults become the calm, confident presence their children need most.

What is JCI?

Junior Chamber International (JCI) is a global nonprofit organisation of young active citizens aged 18–40, present in over 100 countries. It develops leadership, social responsibility and entrepreneurship through local chapter programs and national competitions. In India, JCI chapters invite recognised experts to train, inspire and mentor their members and the wider community. Amandeep Sethi has contributed to JCI India as a speaker, trainer and evaluator — bringing her expertise in conscious parenting, emotional wellbeing and family relationships to audiences of young leaders and community members across 20+ cities.
